BlackJack

Wellcome to BlackJack!

This is the classic blackjack game. Enter your name and credit below and click "Ok".

Click "Deal" to deal the cards. Click "Hit" to add another card to your hand. Try to get as close as possible to 21, but not over because you will lose. When you are satisfied with the value of your hands, click "Stand" and wait for the dealer's turn.
Whoever has the highest score wins.
